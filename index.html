AcidMod

AcidMod is a powerful visual IDE for game creators.


Create your creations visually

Create your apps, games, websites, everything in the whathow visually. Based off Scratch and TurboWarp, you can code your things by snapping together blocks and seeing them on the stage. You can work with multiple resolutions by opening the "Advanced" Menu by selecting the "A" icon on the studio menubar.


Speed.

With the javascript compilation engine of TurboWarp, we at AcidMod applied numerous patches to the editor to make it faster than ever, such as not drawing sprites when they are offscreen.


Export to your platforms

Using the powerful AcidMod Exporter from the File menu, you can export your project to various formats, and customize the nitty gritty options of it.


Your editor, your way.

Change your accents and block styles, dark and light mode, and the Addons functionality really lets you customize the editor the way YOU want.


So, what are you waiting for?
